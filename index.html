AboutTobi Adebayo

Tobi Adebayo (born Adebayo Oluwatobi James, 2000) is a visual artist renowned for his intriguing and thought-provoking works that inspire unity, hope and Faith (UHF). Born and raised in Ibadan, Nigeria, before moving to another city at age 9. Tobi’s creative journey began at a tender age, nurtured by his family’s rich artistic heritage, where everyone has their diverse creative abilities.
